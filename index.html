Privacy Policy

Last updated: 08 Oct 2024

This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how you can exercise your rights under GDPR.

Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • App usage data: We collect information about how users interact with the app, such as the frequency of visits to specific pages and overall usage patterns.
  • Grocery history data: For users who choose to sign in with their Google account, we collect data about finished grocery shopping sessions. This includes items purchased, quantities, prices, and the date of the shopping trip. This data is collected solely to provide you with a grocery shopping history feature.

It's important to note that grocery history data is only collected if you choose to sign in with your Google account. If you use the app without signing in, no grocery history data will be collected or stored on our servers.

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data in the following ways:

  • App usage data is used to improve user experience and optimize the app's functionality by understanding which features are most popular.
  • Grocery history data is used exclusively to provide you with a history of your past shopping trips, allowing you to review and analyze your grocery shopping habits over time.

Your Rights Under GDPR

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to erase your data
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object

To exercise these rights, please contact us at openlabmrz@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

Contact Us
